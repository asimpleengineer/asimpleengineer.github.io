Share memory by communicating

"Do not communicate by sharing memory"

The most subjective, personal principle and one of my favorite Golang proverbs.

Take this principle with a grain of salt since it might not apply to every design decision. However, in 90% of your applications, applying this principle will save you a lot of time down the road. Dealing with state/memory is not easy.
