III

Just go do it!

The hard part is to start.

It happens to us all. As multiple studies have shown, procrastination is real.

Just before starting this list of principles, I thought to myself: "Why do it? No one will read it", "This doesn't bring any value to most engineers". The reason is, we come up with multiple excuses to start something and we delay, and delay....

If you don't start, no one will for you. So the only option you have is to do the actual work. I like to think that if it's hard to do it, do it twice. Going back to these principles, I honestly did it to myself and to remind me about the core principles I vow to follow and never forget.

Do the work. Don't overthink.
